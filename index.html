Instructions

First, you type in a number 1 - 100 into the box with the number 1 in it. After that, click the "Generate Values" button.

How It Works

Once you type in your number and click the "Generate Values" button, random numbers 1-100 will start generating. They will keep on generating until one of the numbers matches what you typed in the box as your number.

Score and Highscore

Your Score is the amount of random numbers it took until it matched your selected number. Your Highscore is the highest random amount of numbers it took until it matched your selected number.
